# don't buy the jeans

A Chrome extension that shows you what a purchase could be worth if you invested it instead.

Enter the cost of something you're thinking of buying and see projected returns at 1, 5, 10, and 30 years — based on the S&P 500's historical average annual return.

---

## How to Use

1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Type in the price of what you're about to buy
3. See what that money could become

---

## Disclaimer

This tool is for informational and entertainment purposes only. It is not financial advice. The projections shown use a 7% average annual return based on historical S&P 500 performance. Past market performance does not guarantee future results. Actual investment returns will vary. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
